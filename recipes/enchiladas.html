Enchiladas Recipe

Description

These delicious enchiladas were created with the finest ingredients. These ingredients have been imported from Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chile sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat you oven to 400 degrees
  2. Chop up the chicken
  3. Place the chicken inside of the tortilla
  4. Add cheese and sauce on top of the chicken
  5. Roll your tortillas and place in a pan
  6. Add cheese, chile sauce, and cilantro to the top of your enchiladas
  7. Place your pan in the oven for 15 minutes
