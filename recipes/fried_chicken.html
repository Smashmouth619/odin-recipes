Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Southern style fried chicken will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat vegetable oil in a pan to 300 degrees
  2. Add your egg and your flour to separate bowls
  3. Dip your chicken in the egg
  4. Next, dip it in the flour
  5. Place your chicken in the pan for 12 minutes
